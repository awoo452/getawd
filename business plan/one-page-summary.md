# AWDevelopment One-Page Plan

## What We Do
AWDevelopment builds production-ready web applications and business sites with a focus on reliability, maintainability, and clear delivery. The core stack is Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, and AWS, plus AWS Amplify for fast, low-budget marketing sites.

## Mission and Vision
Mission: Build practical, production-ready web software that stays simple to maintain, ships predictably, and holds up under real-world use.
Vision: Become the trusted, low-drama development partner for businesses that value clear thinking, stable infrastructure, and systems that keep working as they grow.

## Who We Serve
- Small to mid-sized businesses that need a low-budget startup site that can expand
- Businesses that need modernization or fixes on an existing site
- Local service providers with scheduling or booking needs
- Teams with existing Rails apps that need stabilization

## Why Us
- Practical, no-hype engineering
- Clear scope and predictable delivery
- Full lifecycle support: build, deploy, maintain
- Production-ready infrastructure from day one (HTTPS, backups, monitoring options)

## Packages (Typical)
- Starter Site: $500–$1,500+, AWS Amplify-based marketing site with fast CDN delivery, managed SSL, custom domain, Git-based deploys, and rollbacks
- Growth Site: $3,500–$7,000, Rails-powered site with CMS or editable content, basic workflows, deployed on Heroku, includes 1 year of Basic maintenance
- Custom Rails App: $8,000–$25,000+, full-stack Rails app with managed PostgreSQL, staging + production, automated deploys, logs, backups, monitoring, and rollbacks, includes 1 year of Pro maintenance

## Ongoing Support
- Basic: $100/month (hosting, maintenance updates, routine backups)
- Pro: $300/month (hosting, maintenance updates, routine backups, unlimited* S3 data storage)

## How We Sell

- Direct identification of businesses with workflow pain
