# AWDevelopment Business Plan (Expanded)

## Executive Summary
AWDevelopment is a solo web development studio led by Aaron, a Washington State-based developer who builds production-ready web software with an emphasis on maintainability, clarity, and predictable delivery. The company focuses on Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, and AWS, with a secondary offering of fast, low-budget static or hybrid sites deployed on AWS Amplify. The immediate goal is to deliver reliable web projects for small to mid-sized businesses that need a clean startup site that can expand over time or a modernization pass on an existing site.

## Company Overview
- Business name: AWDevelopment
- Owner: Aaron (solo operator)
- Location: Washington State, USA
- Geographic focus: US-based clients, remote-first, some ability to travel
- Core stack: Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, AWS
- Positioning: Practical, long-term maintainable software with clear delivery and stable infrastructure
- Operating model: Lean, low-overhead studio with a repeatable delivery process

## Mission and Vision
- Mission: Build practical, production-ready web software that stays simple to maintain, ships predictably, and holds up under real-world use.
- Vision: Become the trusted, low-drama development partner for businesses that value clear thinking, stable infrastructure, and systems that keep working as they grow.

## Problem and Opportunity
Many small to mid-sized businesses need a web presence or internal tooling that is reliable, expandable, and not overloaded with fragile plugins or short-lived technology choices. They often face one of two problems.

- They need a simple site now but want a foundation that can evolve into a real application.
- They have an existing app that is too brittle or under-maintained to safely improve.

AWDevelopment addresses this with a Rails-first approach for long-term stability and a fast static-site option when speed and budget are the highest priorities.

## Target Customers
- Small to mid-sized businesses that need a low-budget startup site that can expand
- Businesses that need modernization or fixes on an existing site
- Service businesses that require online booking, scheduling, or order intake
- Teams that have an existing Rails app but need stabilization or ongoing improvements

## Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)
- Local service providers with recurring client workflows, typically 3–25 employees, who need scheduling, intake, or quoting workflows.
- Owner-led businesses that want a straightforward site now and a clear path to a custom app later.
- Teams with a Rails app that needs refactoring, performance tuning, or support without switching stacks.

## Packaging and Typical Timelines
AWDevelopment sells clear, fixed-scope packages that make outcomes predictable.

- Starter Site: $500–$1,500+, 1 week delivery time, AWS Amplify-based marketing site
- Growth Site: $3,500–$7,000, 1-2 weeks, expanded Rails-first site with basic CMS and related workflow features
- Custom Rails App: $8,000–$25,000+, 3-6+ weeks depending on scope
- Stabilization Sprint: $3,000–$6,000+, 1-2 weeks, bug triage, performance, and modernization

## Package Details
These are the typical outcomes for each tier. Exact scope is confirmed in discovery.

- Starter Site: 1-3 key pages, contact form, basic analytics, simple SEO setup, fast turnaround, minimal content input required from client. Includes AWS Amplify hosting benefits like global CDN delivery, managed SSL and HTTPS, custom domain setup, Git-based automated deploys, branch previews, and one-click rollbacks.
- Growth Site: 5-10 pages, CMS or editable content areas including image gallery support, basic workflows or intake forms, improved SEO structure, deployed on Heroku with a basic data storage plan. Powered by Rails and includes 1 year of Basic maintenance.
- Custom Rails App: user accounts, admin dashboard, database-backed workflows, integrations, and a clear foundation for iteration. Includes production-ready Rails hosting on Heroku or AWS with managed PostgreSQL, HTTPS, staging and production environments, automated deploys, logs, backups, monitoring, and rollback options. Includes 1 year of Pro maintenance.
- Stabilization Sprint: prioritized backlog, performance fixes, dependency updates, and a short written handoff.

## Pricing Model
AWDevelopment uses fixed-scope pricing to provide predictable costs for clients and avoid hourly billing uncertainty.

- Static business websites (AWS Amplify): $500–$3,000
- Expanded business websites: $3,500–$7,000
- Custom Rails applications: $8,000–$25,000+
- Technical Consulting (virtual): $88/hour (2-hour minimum)
- Technical Consulting (On Site): $150/hour + travel

Clients may optionally enroll in monthly hosting and maintenance packages ranging from $100–$500 per month depending on support level.
Standard payment terms are 50 percent deposit, 40 percent mid-project, 10 percent at launch, unless a milestone plan is agreed in writing.

Pricing reflects a production-ready foundation, not just a visual build. The goal is a site or system that performs well, is stable under real use, and can grow without a rebuild.

## Maintenance Packages
- Basic: $100/month (hosting, maintenance updates, routine backups)
- Pro: $300/month (hosting, maintenance updates, routine backups, unlimited* s3 data storage)

*Unlimited storage means no hard storage cap within normal, fair-use expectations. High-volume or large media storage is priced separately and should be disclosed when intended storage exceeds 1 TB of data.
